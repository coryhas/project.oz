Ozzy Osbourne and Randy Rhoades Tribute



Quick History

TRhoades served as Osbourne's guitarist from 1979 to 1982, during which time they recorded the first two albums of the Osbourne's solo career: Blizzard of Ozz and Diary of a Madman. This includes wonderful songs like Crazy Train and Mr. Crowly. Unfortunatly Rhoades passed away in a freak helicopter March 19, 1982 at the age of 25.



Original Members of Ozzy Solo Group

  • Ozzy Osbourne-Singer
  • Randy Rhoades-Guitarist
  • Bob Daisley-Bassist
  • Lee Kerslake-Drummer
